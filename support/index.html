Frequently asked

The camera is black or recording won't start.

VideoNarraCue needs Camera and Microphone access. Open Settings → VideoNarraCue and enable Camera and Microphone. The record button stays disabled until the camera is ready.

Where are my recordings saved?

Finished clips are saved straight to your iPhone's Photos library. Make sure you allow “Add to Photos” when prompted.

Where do the auto-captions go?

When enabled (Pro), an .srt subtitle file is generated on-device and saved to Files → On My iPhone → VideoNarraCue. Captions work for languages supported for on-device speech and are best recorded in a quiet space.

How do I change the video format or resolution?

Use the 9:16 / 1:1 / 16:9 selector on the main screen, and choose resolution (up to 4K on Pro) in Settings. The preview shows exactly how the clip will be cropped.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your device. Use “Restore Purchases” in the app's paywall if a purchase isn't recognized.

Can I import or scan a script?

Yes — open the script drawer to import a PDF/TXT, or tap “Scan” to capture a printed page with on-device OCR.
