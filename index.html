Privacy by design

Your content never leaves your device

There are no servers receiving your videos, scripts or audio. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking.

  • Recordings are saved to your own Photos library.
  • Captions and speech run on-device with Apple's frameworks.
  • Scripts and settings are stored locally on your iPhone.
  • No third-party trackers, ads, or data collection.
